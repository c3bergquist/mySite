• What's with all of the video game references?

    First of all, I don't appreciate the hostility. This is my website, and I'll do what I'd like!

    Each of the games featured on this site have had some level of impact on me that directly affected my path in life.

  • Super Mario Bros.

    If my parents hadn't "lost" our old NES, I'd probably still be playing this game to this very day. Super Mario Bros. was my first real trip into the world of video games.

    So, when it really comes down to it, my parents are responsible for getting me addicted to video games.

  • The Legend of Zelda: Majora's Mask

    I can say without question that this is my favorite game of all time.

    The eerie and totally story. The super creepy visuals. The INCREDIBLE soundtrack. Sure, it's mostly the same assets as Ocarina of Time. But I didn't know that when I got it, and I didn't care when I found out.

  • Minecraft

    "Here's a bunch of cubes. Go build something. Or don't. Whatever."

    This was basically "LEGOs - The Video Game" before LEGO actually had a LEGO video game made. There was no story. There was no directive. You were - quite literally - dropped into a randomly generated world where you could either scavenge for supplies for a survival mode, or go into creative and just build whatever you wanted.

    I built a 1:1 model of a NASA Orbiter. Because of course I did.

  • Portal

    Slightly behind the times on this one, I found out about Portal my Freshman year of college (2010, only 3 years late). I walked into one of the common areas and someone was playing it up on the big TV in the room.

    This game BLEW MY MIND.
